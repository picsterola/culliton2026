Submit a response

Candidates, campaigns, and members of the public are invited to submit corrections, clarifications, or additional public-record context. Documented submissions will be reviewed and, where appropriate, appended to the relevant candidate page.

We are particularly interested in:

  • Citations to public statements, writings, or judicial opinions that bear on the underlying constitutional question.
  • Corrections to factual claims about a candidate's biography, appointing authority, or record.
  • Links to upcoming public forums or bar-association questionnaires.
  • Photo substitution requests from candidates or rights-holders.

We do not publish anonymous tips, opposition research, or unsourced claims.
